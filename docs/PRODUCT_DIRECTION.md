# Product direction

**Chosen path: C — parity investment** (see internal plan “Sheets vs platform parity”).

The web app should become a **trustworthy, easy-to-navigate** companion to the Job Numbering workbook: same job-level and rollup numbers where possible, a **simple default path** (year → AM summary → jobs), and **Advanced** for commissions, payroll, Excel snapshots, and deep reports.

Sheets may remain in use for edge workflows until parity checks pass; the codebase targets **import accuracy + GP policy + navigation** as one roadmap.
